ShelterSafe



Idea
ShelterSafe is a mobile app designed to guide users to Shelters in the case of emergency. Taking advantage of live data from nearby shelters and algorithms that generate possible outcomes and the ideal shelter, it will revolutionize safety. For the technology used, we intend to begin our project using Xcode and continue to develop an Android version in Kotlin should time allow. There'll be a backend to store data and the map, and a frontend that'll bring a simplistic UI. Admin permissions to edit the backend will be given to local organizations and governments, who can update the live data for shelters that they run. At just a simple touch of a phone screen, lives can be saved.
